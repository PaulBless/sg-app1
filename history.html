The Sandia Journey

Our Story

In the bustling heart of West Africa, a visionary entrepreneur "Sami Houdrouge" embarked on a journey to redefine excellence across industries. Thus, in February 2006, Sandia Oil was born in the vibrant nation of Cote D’ivoire. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to integrity, Sandia Oil swiftly established itself as a beacon of reliability in the realm of oil bunkering and trading.

As the sands of time continued to shift, the Sandia Group of Companies expanded its horizons, nurturing a family of subsidiaries that shared its unwavering dedication to progress and prosperity.

In 2021, Sandia Agric was founded, the agricultural arm of the conglomerate, sowing the seeds of transformation across the supply chain. From importing and distributing cutting-edge agro-chemicals and seeds to empowering small-scale farmers, Sandia Agric became synonymous with growth and sustainability, forging win-win partnerships that blossomed into bountiful harvests.

Meanwhile, Sandia Energy emerged on the scene in March 2015, its roots firmly planted in the rich soil of Ghana. With a focus on brokerage services and the procurement of Oil & Gas products, Sandia Energy became a vital conduit for global commerce, connecting suppliers and consumers with seamless efficiency.

In February 2021, Sandia Engineering joined the ranks, poised to revolutionize Ghana's civil infrastructure sector. Armed with state-of-the-art technologies and a steadfast commitment to excellence, Sandia Engineering forged ahead, reshaping skylines and paving the way for a brighter future.

Not to be outdone, Sansar Innovations emerged in 2020, a beacon of creativity and technological prowess in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. With a focus on software development and cutting-edge innovations, Sansar Innovations charted new frontiers, transforming ideas into reality with unparalleled precision.

And finally, in 2022, Sandia Commodities stepped onto the stage, poised to conquer the world of commodities trading with its unwavering commitment to quality and integrity.

Together, these subsidiaries form the pillars of the Sandia Group of Companies, each contributing its unique strengths to a shared vision of excellence and innovation. As the sands of time continue to shift and the winds of change blow ever stronger, the Sandia Group stands firm, a beacon of hope and opportunity in an ever-changing world.

Sami Houdrouge, CEO
